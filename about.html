NUCIFERA Virgin Coconut Oil

NUCIFERA VCO is pure, cold pressed, unrefined oil extracted from coconut milk. The cold process method ensures that all the vital nutrients and natural goodness of fresh coconuts are retained. We at NUCIFERA, follow stringent measures at every stage to bring to you a virgin coconut oil of the highest quality

NUCIFERA Difference - Not All VCOs are the Same

A lot of the virgin coconut oils in the market are made by expeller method from dehydrated coconut meat, whereas, NUCIFERA VCO is extracted from fresh coconut milk making it the finest quality of virgin coconut oil. It does not contain any additives/preservatives
